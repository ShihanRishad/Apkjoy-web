Introduction

What is ApkJoy?

ApkJoy is your app on Android, which lets you control the apps on your phone. It can help you share the app APKs that you can't find on app stores, extract them, or play around with them!

Why use ApkJoy?

ApkJoy is designed to be simple, fast, and secure. It doesn't require any permissions, it saves your extracted APKs in apps' folder.

About ApkJoy

Features

ApkJoy offers a range of features to enhance your app experience. Some of the key features include:
  • Extract: Save an extract the APK of the apps on your phone.
  • Share: Quickly share your extracted apps in one tap
  • Customizability: Make the app yours, change to your own theme and color.
  • Lightweight: ApkJoy is designed to be lightweight and fast, ensuring a smooth user experience.
  • Open Source: ApkJoy is open source, allowing users to contribute and improve the app.
  • Privacy: ApkJoy does not collect any personal data or require unnecessary permissions.
  • Free Forever: No sign in, no payment, just use it.

Compatibility

ApkJoy is compatible with all Android devices running Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and above.
