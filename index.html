Introduction

What is ApkJoy?

ApkJoy is your app on Android, which lets you control the apps on your phone. It can help you share the app APKs that you can't find on app stores, extract them, or play around with them!

Why use ApkJoy?

ApkJoy is designed to be simple, fast, and secure. It doesn't require any permissions, it saves your extracted APKs in apps' folder.

Features

Extract APKs

ApkJoy allows you to extract the APK files of the apps installed on your device. This feature is useful for backing up apps or sharing them with others.

Share APKs

With ApkJoy, you can easily share the extracted APK files with your friends or family. This feature makes it convenient to share apps without relying on third-party app stores.

Customizability

ApkJoy offers a range of customization options, allowing you to personalize the app according to your preferences. You can change themes, colors, and more.

Search

ApkJoy provides a powerful search feature that allows you to quickly find the apps you are looking for. You can search by app name.

Lightweight

ApkJoy is made with flutter, and designed to be lightweight and fast, ensuring a smooth user experience. It doesn't consume unnecessary resources, making it suitable for most devices.

Open Source

ApkJoy is an open-source project, allowing users to contribute to its development. You can find the source code on GitHub and participate in improving the app.

Privacy

ApkJoy respects your privacy. It does not collect any personal data or require unnecessary permissions. Your data is safe with ApkJoy.

Free Forever

ApkJoy is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Enjoy the app without any restrictions.

Help

Having issues?

If you're having issues, checkout our documentation. Here are somethings to check if issues happening while install:

  • Make sure you have enabled "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device's settings.
  • Check if your device has enough storage space for the installation.
  • If you encounter any errors, try restarting your device and then attempt the installation again.
  • This app might not work on older android devices.
